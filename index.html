About Dochuma Academy

At Dochuma Academy, our mission is simple — to impact every child with the ability to solve mathematical equations quickly, accurately, and confidently.

We believe every child has a spark of genius within them, and through our structured Abacus and Mental Mathematics programs, we nurture that potential into excellence.

Our approach combines traditional teaching with modern techniques that improve focus, memory, and problem-solving skills. Beyond numbers, our students learn discipline, creativity, and self-belief — life skills that extend far beyond the classroom.

Our Programs

Abacus Foundation

Introduces children to the ancient Abacus technique, helping them visualize numbers and perform arithmetic operations with remarkable speed.

Advanced Mental Arithmetic

Designed for older students who wish to master complex calculations mentally, preparing them for academic excellence and competitive exams

We collaborate with schools to integrate our curriculum and train educators in the Dochuma methodology — ensuring every classroom benefits from our system.
